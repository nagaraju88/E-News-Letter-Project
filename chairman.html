About Us

Education is all about creating an environment of academic freedom, where bright minds meet,discover and learn.
Balaji Institute of Technology & Science is the one of the best Engineering college in warangal district, popularly known as BITS, was established by Maheshwara Educational Society in 2001 at Laknepally Village near Narsampet beside Warangal-Narsampet Main Road in an area of 35 acres with an in take of 180.
The place is well connected by road and train and is only 20 minutes drive from Warangal.
Presently the college offers B.Tech courses in EEE, ME, ECE, CIVIL & CSE with an intake of 660 besides MBA with an intake of 60.

CHAIRMAN'S DESK

Dear parents and prospective students,
Welcome to Balaji Institute of Technology & Science. Education is a very important contributing element to both social mobility and economic development. Technological progress in the last few decades in communication, transportation and information has helped to eliminate national barriers and create a global market place. We wish to introduce ourselves as the promoters of Balaji Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) which was established in 2001, with a view to provide quality technical education to young rural students. It gives me an immense pleasure, that the college has come a long way from its inception batch of 2001, in providing not only technical knowledge, but also the overall growth and development of the budding stars of future India. The rapid rate of technological advancement and the information revolution have opened new series of challenges as well as opportunities. BITS aim to prepare the students to be successful in the changing workplace. It aims to equip students with technical knowledge and capabilities, flexibility and an understanding of the societal context of corporate world. We give the students support and encouragement they need to reach their full potential. Today in the era of liberalization, privatization & globalization we try to groom nourish & nurture our students in such a way that they are fully equipped to meet the global demands. We strive to give professional education a new perspective and achieve perfection in all spheres. We provide a pleasant & intellectually stimulating environment. The main reason for our tremendous performance is the faculty, which makes BITS stand out from the rest of Engineering colleges and Institutes. We provide exposure to latest and advanced Techniques and system. We gave the world the best we have and the best we can.
